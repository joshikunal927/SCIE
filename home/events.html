INTRODUCTION:

In this dynamic world, Technology plays a significant role in the learning paradigm. The youth of today is brimming with curiosity and inquisitiveness. They are perpetually on the move. So, in order to match them with the rapid pace of advancement and the demands of industry, Technical events like extempore, debates, Group discussions, quizzes, guest-talks and many others, are organized throughout the year for students of all batches and streams to enhance their skills. Through these events, SCIE strives to bring out the hidden talents in the students. SCIE actively participates in the organization of college level and national level symposiums. It contributes massively in Technical Fests like Genesis and Technovision.
