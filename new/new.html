WELCOME DEAR FELLOW ENGINEERS!

The Student's Chapter of The Institution of Engineers (GNDEC) invites you to join this prestigious organization. Membership opens avenues for professional growth and knowledge assimilation. Join us to serve our country and achieve glory.

With over a century of service, the Institution has a strong national and international presence through numerous centers and chapters. It encompasses various engineering disciplines and offers professional certifications.

The Institution of Engineers (India) represents India in several international bodies and conducts examinations recognized as equivalent to engineering degrees. It also publishes peer-reviewed journals and organizes technical activities.

HISTORY

Genesis: Post-World War I, industrialization in India led to the need for quality assurance in engineering. The Government of India formed the Indian Industrial Commission, which recommended establishing a professional engineering institution.
The Indian Society of Engineers was formed in 1919, later renamed The Institution of Engineers (India). The organization has since grown to support engineering excellence and professional development.
